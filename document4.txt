"I don't want them to stop my father," a child whispers in Spanish. "I don't want them to deport him."
So begins an excruciating audio clip of children howling for their parents after being separated from them at the US-Mexico border.
The audio was published Monday by the investigative nonprofit ProPublica.
It adds a visceral element to the coverage of a controversial Trump administration policy of taking undocumented immigrant children
from their families and putting them in government facilities on US borders.
